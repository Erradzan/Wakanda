Tuition Breakdown

  1. Elementary School (Grades 1-5): $10,000 - $15,000 per year.
  2. Middle School (Grades 6-8): $15,000 - $20,000 per year.
  3. High School (Grades 9-12): $20,000 - $25,000 per year.

Additional Cost

  1. Enrollment Fee: $500 - $1,000 (one-time).
  2. Books and Supplies: $500 - $1,000 per year.
  3. Extracurricular Activities: $500 - $2,000 per year (depending on the activities).
  4. Uniforms: $200 - $500 per year.
  5. Boarding (if applicable): $10,000 - $20,000 per year.

Financial Aid Structure.

Tuition Fee Structure: Full Tuition: $10,000 - $25,000 per year (depending on grade level).

Financial Aid Tiers:

  1. Tier 1: 90% aid - Families with income below $3,500 per year.
  2. Tier 2: 75% aid - Families with income between $3,500 - $7,000 per year.
  3. Tier 3: 50% aid - Families with income between $7,000 - $10,000 per year.
  4. Tier 4: 25% aid - Families with income between $10,000 - $14,000 per year.

Merit-Based Scholarships:

  1. Academic Excellence Scholarship: 25-100% tuition coverage.
  2. Artistic Talent Scholarship: 25-100% tuition coverage.
  3. Athletic Achievement Scholarship: 25-100% tuition coverage.

Work-Study Program:

Student Jobs: Up to 20 hours per week, earning $100 per month to offset tuition costs.

Enhanced Student Loan Program Structure for Wakanda Academy.

Loan Terms and Conditions:

  1. Eligibility: Defined based on financial need, academic potential, and commitment to repay.
  2. Loan Amount: Covers full or partial tuition and additional expenses like books and supplies.
  3. Interest Rate: No interest (0%).
  4. Repayment Period: Set a reasonable repayment period, such as 5-10 years.
  5. Grace Period: Up to 3-4 years after graduation, allowing ample time for students to secure stable employment.

Repayment Options:

  1. Standard Repayment Plan: Fixed monthly payments over the repayment period.
  2. Income-Based Repayment Plan: Payments as a percentage of the borrower's income, ensuring affordability.
  3. Deferment and Forbearance Options: Allow temporary pauses in repayment for students facing financial hardship.

Example of the Interest-Free Student Loan Program.

Loan Terms:

  1. Eligibility: Students from families with income below $10,000 per year.
  2. Loan Amount: Up to $25,000 per student.
  3. Interest Rate: 0% (interest-free).
  4. Repayment Period: 10 years.
  5. Grace Period: Up to 3-4 years post-graduation.
